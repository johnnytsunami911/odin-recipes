Blue Milk!

Description

I alway wanted to try the blue milk from the Star Wars franchise. It was a staple food item from it and i am finally old enough to make it on my own.

Feast your eyes on the incredible BLUE MILK!

Ingredients

  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Passion Fruit Juice
  • Lime Juice
  • Coconut Syrup
  • Melon Syrup
  • Blue Food Coloring

Steps

  1. First step is to combine all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Then blend until it has a creamy and smooth texture.
  3. Finally, pour and serve with whip cream and a cherry on top.
